Welded wire mesh

Welded wire mesh is made of high quality iron wire throught automatic process and sophisticated welding technique,laid horzontally and vertically.Individually welded at every intersection. The end products is level and flat with sturdystructure.The finished welded wire mesh offer flat and uniform surface firm structure, good integrity.

Features of Welded Wire Mesh:

  • Welded by high quality iron wire.
  • Adaptable for all fencing needs and DIY .
  • Remains perfectly straight and flat.
  • Smooth and uniform mesh edge.
  • Welded cross wires for added strength.
  • Uniform bright galvanizing.
  • Extreme corrosion resistant.
  • Easy to handle and carry.
  • Easy to install.
